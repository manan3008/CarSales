About Hyundai Aura

Hyundai Aura, a Sedan, is available from ₹ 6.95 Lakh to ₹ 8.37 Lakh in Australia. Hyundai offers the latest Aura in 10 versions in Australia. The base model E 1.2 Petrol starts at ₹ 6.95 Lakh in sydney and the top model SX Plus 1.2 AMT CRDi is priced at ₹ 8.37 Lakh in sydney. If you are looking for 5 seater car then you should consider Aura.
Aura comes in 7 Petrol version(s), 4 Diesel version(s), 1 CNG version(s).
The Petrol engine is offered in 2 different variants. First Petrol version comes with 1197cc engine capacity and both manual, automatic transmission mode(s). It generates 81hp power at 6000 rpm.
Second Petrol version comes with 998cc engine capacity and manual transmission mode(s). It generates 99hp power at 6000 rpm. Diesel version comes with 1186cc engine capacity and both manual, automatic transmission mode(s). It generates 74hp power at 4000 rpm.
CNG version comes with 1197cc engine capacity and manual transmission mode(s). It generates 81hp power at 6000 rpm.
Hyundai Aura comes in 6 colors which include Alpha Blue, Titan Grey, Vintage Brown, Typhoon Silver, Fiery Red, Polar White.
